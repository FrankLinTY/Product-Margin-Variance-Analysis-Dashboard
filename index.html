Methodology

This dashboard decomposes the period-over-period movement in overall product margin rate into three category-level effects: Mix Effect, Volume Effect, and Rate Effect.

Mix Effect captures how changes in each product category's proportional share of total sales volume affect the weighted-average margin rate.

Volume Effect captures how changes in the absolute sales volume within each category affect the overall margin rate, independent of its share change.

Rate Effect captures how changes in each category's own margin rate affect the overall weighted average.

Impact values are expressed in percentage points. A positive impact indicates the driver lifted the weighted-average rate; a negative impact indicates it dragged the rate down.

This framework is domain-agnostic: the same Mix / Volume / Rate decomposition applies equally to payment success rate, churn rate, conversion rate, or any weighted-average rate metric.

This dashboard uses synthetic data for portfolio demonstration purposes. It does not represent any real company, real product category, or internal business data. Domain originally demonstrated on payment success rate variance; refactored as a reusable variance framework.
